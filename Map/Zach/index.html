I chose this location (Fort Awesome) because it is where I began to record music seriously.

At this location, the path of my life changed directions. While living at Fort Awesome, I started to see myself as a music producer and I began my journey as an artist.

I could not have and would not have embarked on this journey if it were not for my friends who provided me with just enough support and criticism to keep going.

This experience taught me that it is never too late to pursue something that you desire.

If I could leave some advice for the future occupants of this location, I would tell them that if you have a dream, you should start pursuing it, now.
